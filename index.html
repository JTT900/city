Top three activities to do at Tjotaheiti

Rail road museum

Check out trains from a century-old narrow-gauge railway.

Go fast downhill

Skiing is a thrilling experience. Bring your prefered gear up the lift and take off.

Buckle up

Time your trip just right and experience the world renowned winter rally

Did you know?

Tjotaheiti is home to the Lumithrill, a majestic creature whose iridescent scales and melodic wings have guarded the enchanted city nestled deep within the forest.
